Introducing FansPlay

The most fun way to watch Reality TV!

Get into Reality TV like never before!

Watch your favorite TV shows every week and support your favorite contestants like never before! Select the reality stars that you like the most and follow along as they win or lose on TV. When your contestants win so do you!

FansPlay is fun and easy to use!

Join

Download our FansPlay App in the App Store and compete with thousands of other reality TV fans! Pick your favorite reality TV contestants and cheer for them each week.

Play

Compete each week with your friends and family. You can also join celebrity pools to compete against thousands of other fans. Pick the winners each and every week!

Win

When the reality TV stars that you select win, so do you! Swap your contestants if you pick new favorites or keep faithful and earn points as your stars advance!
