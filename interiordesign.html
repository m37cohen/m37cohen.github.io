Interior Design

Continuing the themes of the architectural design, our living spaces are clad in warm birch contrasted by clean modern finishes. The result is visual warmth, and understated sophistication. This reduces the need for artificial lighting by reflecting the daylight from the windows and sources of daylight the spaces are designed around.

Despite an efficient floor plan, our design boasts a double height living room to alleviate the typical cramped feeling of a downtown condo. This space flows into the central staircase, flanked on one side with a birch feature wall which stretches from the main floor all the way to the ceilings.

Another feature differentiating our design from a typical home is the cathedral ceilings in the second floor. Rather than dedicate a large amount of space to an attic, the bedroom ceilings follow the sloping profile of the roof, creating dynamic spaces and contrasting the linear birch walls.
