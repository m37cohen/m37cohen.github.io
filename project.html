Our Project

Our Proposal

Our proposal is a dense urban infill model that places residents in communities surrounded by public transit, street networks, modern utilities, services and amenities. Choosing sites that satisfy these requirements and maintain affordability are integral to our vision.

As a by-product of poor urban planning, several of Toronto’s main avenues are flanked by oddly shaped, undervalued lots. Surrounded by public transit, services, and the cultural boons of the city, these provide the perfect location for our proposal.

What Makes Our Design Unique?

Stackable

The repeatable, stackable family home combats suburban sprawl, and provides the density needed to house our growing population.

Affordable

The home is designed to be affordable to produce. Everything from the walls to the roof can be taken apart, flat-packed and transported in a shipping container.

Sustainable

The design optimizes passive and active strategies to maximize the performance of the building. Every unit is net zero and provides surplus energy to charge electric vehicles.

Livable

Each home is designed to be comfortable and spacious, with an upstairs and a downstairs as well as an entrance off of grade.
