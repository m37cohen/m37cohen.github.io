Our Approach

Paramount Capital Partners is typically focused on the following types of investments, although the fund is always interested in exploring other opportunities that offer attractive return profiles:

  • Distressed debt and businesses
  • Private real estate transactions
  • Entrepreneur-driven small businesses experiencing high growth
  • Interdisciplinary niche private lending
  • Specialty management buy-outs and roll-ups
  • Strong historical, sustainable cash flow return
  • Traditional, long-term businesses with steady track records of success
  • Market opportunity growth and service oriented assets
  • Potential for long-term growth and significant value creation
