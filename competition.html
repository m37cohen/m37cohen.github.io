The Competition

2017 Solar Decathlon China

Originally launched by the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE), the 2017 Solar Decathlon will be taking place in Dezhou - China's 'Solar City'. Over 20 teams from all around the world will be competing to design, build and operate their own version of a full-scale, entirely functional net zero energy home.

The purpose of the competition is to educate students, inform the public and accelerate the adoption of energy-efficient products. The contest will attract hundreds of thousands of visitors and will be extensively covered in the media, both internationally and in the local areas of the competing teams.
