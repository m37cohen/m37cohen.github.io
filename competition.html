The Competition

Solar Decathlon 2017

What?

An international competition where teams design and build function solar homes.

Where?

The competition will be held in Dezhou, China's 'Solar City'.

When?

The event will take place between Month ## and Month ## 2017

Team Toronto is one of the finalists for the 2017 Solar Decathlon China which will be held in Dezhou, China’s ‘Solar City’. We will be competing with teams from across the globe, all of whom will be showcasing cutting edge sustainable technologies and designs. The contest will attract hundreds of thousands of visitors and will be extensively covered in the media, both internationally and in the local areas of the competing teams.
