The Competition

Solar Decathlon 2017

The Solar Decathlon is an international competition where teams design, build, and operate a full-scale, entirely functional net zero energy house. It’s purpose is to educate students, inform the public and accelerate the adoption of energy-efficient products.

Our team is one of the finalists for the 2017 Solar Decathlon China which will be held in Dezhou, China’s ‘Solar City’. We will be competing with teams from across the globe, all of whom will be showcasing cutting edge sustainable technologies and designs. The contest will attract hundreds of thousands of visitors and will be extensively covered in the media, both internationally and in the local areas of the competing teams.
