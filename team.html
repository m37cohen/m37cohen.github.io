Mark Vasey

Mark Vasey currently serves as an Associate at York Plains. Mark is responsible for analysis and recommendations on portfolio investments and has been in the investment analysis field for over 5 years. Mark holds a Honours in Business Administration from the Richard Ivey School of Business and is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) Charterholder.
