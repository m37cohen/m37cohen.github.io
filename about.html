What is ECOstudio?

Who We Are

ECOstudio is an interdisciplinary group of faculty and students from Ryerson University, the University of Toronto and Seneca College. Our team has a collective wealth of experience and expertise in engineering, architecture, interior design and business.

We have come together to design a state-of-the-art, net-zero urban home. Our proposal is for a mid-density architectural design that will make use of highly integrated energy systems, advanced control algorithms and streamlined construction techniques to maximize sustainability, energy efficicency and end cost.
