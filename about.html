About Us

Paramount Capital Partners is a private equity, fund-management firm based in Windsor, Ontario. The firm strategically invests in public and private securities across multiple asset classes. This approach maximizes absolute returns for a select group of high net worth individuals.

The vast majority of Paramount Capital's resources are focused on research and analyses of the North American market. Historically the principals of Paramount Capital have focused on direct sales and the energy sector where past performance has been successful. Currently, the fund has 125MM+of assets under management and is closed to new investment.

The firm leverages a risk/reward proposition for idea generation and to evaluate assets we consider undervalued. Our ability to establish key partnerships and relationships allows Paramount Capital to maximize performance for shareholders.
